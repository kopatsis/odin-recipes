Pickled Pig's Feet

A vintage recipe for a traditional American treat. Pig's feet can be specially ordered from most butcher shops. Don't knock it till you try it!

From justapinch.com

Ingredients:

  • 4 good-sized boiled pigs feet with uppers
  • 1 quart vinegar
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoons whole cloves
  • 1 tablespoons broken cinnamon sticks
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 2 teaspoons pepper
  • 1/2 large onion, cut into eights
  • 1 blade of mace
